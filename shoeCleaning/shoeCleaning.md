#How clean shoes
## Clean Nubuk Shoes
- **Source:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=JBhVijS6_Do
### Preparation
1. Remove shoe laces
2. Apply shoe tree
### Cleaning the upper
1. Brush the shoe with nubuk brush `rubber side` to remove dirt
2. Use `nubuk eraser` to remove stains
3. Brush with general shoe brusher
### Wet cleaning
1. Apply nubuk cleaner foam with sponge/rug or brush
### Cleaning the outer sole
1. Apply outer sole cleaner with a rug and leave it for 3 minutes
2. Brush it with nubuk brush `hard side`
3. Wipe it with a wattered rug
4. Let it dry
### Clean the laces
1. Soak the laces in warm water with liquid soap and brush it with nubuk brush `hard side`
### Brushing suede
1. Brush the suede with nubuk brush `hard side` until the soft texture returns
2. Apply slightly oil spray to restore color
3. Let it dry
### Impregnate - optional
1. Apply nubuk/suede protector spray evenly from 20 cm distance - I did not find this on CCC website available
